Our Cabinet Process

From design concept to final installation — we handle every step.

1

Design Consultation

We visit your space, discuss your vision and budget, and help you select the right cabinet style, finish, and layout for your needs.

2

Order & Fabrication

Your cabinets are ordered and crafted to your specifications. This phase typically takes 8–12 weeks depending on the product line.

3

Expert Installation

Our installation team arrives fully prepared to fit your new cabinets precisely, cleanly, and on schedule.

Cabinet Project Timeline

Custom cabinet projects carry an 8–12 week product lead time from the time your order is placed. Installation follows delivery and typically takes 1–5 days depending on project scope. We’ll provide a complete timeline estimate during your consultation.
