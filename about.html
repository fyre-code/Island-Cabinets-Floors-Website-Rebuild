Our Story

About Island Cabinets & Floors

Veteran-owned. Community-focused. Built on integrity, loyalty, and a commitment to excellence.

Over 30 Years of Transforming Brevard County Homes

Island Cabinets & Floors was founded on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves honest advice, quality craftsmanship, and a result they're proud of. Owner Shane Dooley has spent more than three decades building that reputation in Melbourne and the surrounding Brevard County communities.

As a veteran-owned business, we carry the values of service and accountability into every project. We don't cut corners. We don't oversell. We listen to what you want, guide you through your options, and deliver an installation that lasts.

From a single room of luxury vinyl plank to a full kitchen cabinet renovation, we treat every project — large or small — with the same level of care and professionalism.
