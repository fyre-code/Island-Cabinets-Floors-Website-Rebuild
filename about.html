About Island Cabinets and Floors

Veteran-owned and serving Brevard County for over 30 years, Island Cabinets and Floors helps homeowners and businesses upgrade their spaces with cabinets and flooring built for durability, function, and style.

We offer a wide selection of cabinet and flooring options—carefully chosen for quality and long-term performance—and guide you through the process with straightforward advice and attention to detail.

Our work is rooted in the values we served with: integrity, loyalty, and a commitment to excellence.

Visit our showroom at 297 N. Babcock Street in Melbourne, Florida, and let’s bring your design vision to life.
